In my current job, I've built a web application for the U.S. government to manage their documents, a web-based workflow for a government contractor to speed up an approval process, an Outlook clone for potential pharmaceutical companies to manage adverse events emails, and other products that I had my hands in. But sometimes it's hard for me to tell myself that what I do is actually important since none of the products are consumer based. Worse, sometimes I still question my career path and all that I've learned and yet to be learned. So I decided to create this post to reinforce my love of learning, especially in the technology field.
